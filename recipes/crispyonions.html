Crispy Onions Recipe


description: A simple and delicious recipe for crispy onions that can be used as a topping for burgers, salads, or as a snack on their own.

    ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • avocado oil cooking spray
    Directions:
  1. Preheat an air fryer to 375°F (190°C) according to manufacturer's instructions
  2. Combine flour, cayenne pepper, and salt in a bowl. add onion slices and toss until well coated
  3. Place onions in basket and spray with avocado oil. Air fry until golden brown, 18 to 23 minutes, shaking basket every 4 to 5 minutes
