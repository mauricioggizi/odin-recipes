Hoisin Salmon Recipe


description: A delicious and easy-to-make salmon dish with a sweet and savory hoisin glaze. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a special occasion.
    ingredients:
  • 1 lb salmon fillets
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onions for garnish (optional)
    instructions:
  1. Preheat the air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together hoisin sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
  3. Pat salmon dry and season with salt and pepper. Brush the glaze over the top and sides.
